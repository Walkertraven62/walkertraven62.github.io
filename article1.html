Navi Wins CS2 Paris Major

The inaugural Counter-Strike 2 Major in Copenhagen, Denmark has been won by NAVI, who defeated FaZe Clan 2-1 in the championship match. With this victory, NAVI becomes the first-ever CS:GO global champion, but they had also won the CS:GO Stockholm Major in 2021, thus they are not new to the top of the Counter-Strike scene.
